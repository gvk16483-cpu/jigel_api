Program Structure

Phase I: Wipro-Aligned Industry-Oriented Technical Training
Participants will undergo intensive, instructor-led technical training aligned with Wipro frameworks and prevailing global industry standards. The curriculum covers both foundational and advanced technical concepts and incorporates hands-on laboratory exercises, case-based learning methodologies, and continuous academic and technical mentoring. Training sessions will be conducted from Monday to Friday through Zoom or Google Meet.

Phase II: Wipro-Aligned Internship and Project Execution
This phase emphasizes experiential, application-driven learning through a structured internship model guided by experienced industry professionals. Participants will complete one minor project and one major group project, gaining direct exposure to enterprise-grade workflows, collaborative development environments, and industry-standard tools, technologies, and methodologies.

Phase III: Placement Support and Career Readiness
Comprehensive placement and career-readiness support will be provided, including resume and professional profile optimization, mock technical and HR interviews, group discussion preparation, communication and interpersonal skills development, and personalized career mentoring aimed at maximizing employability and placement outcomes.
